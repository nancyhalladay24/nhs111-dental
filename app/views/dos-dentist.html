Things to look out for and self-care

Toothache

  • Don't apply gels or medicine directly to the tooth or nearby gum area. This may damage gums.
  • Avoid very hot or cold food and drink if it makes the pain worse
  • Chew on the other side of your mouth
  • See a dentist for your toothache. Any other cures will be temporary

Medication, pain and/or fever

  • Paracetamol or ibuprofen can be used to relieve pain or a fever. Don't take if you've been told not to, or you've already taken some. Read the instructions on the packet, or talk to a pharmacist.
What to do if you start to feel worse
  1. Call 111
  2. Tell them 111 online said you needed to get help within 1 hour.
  3. Tell them your symptoms have got worse and you need to speak to a dental nurse.

Things to look out for and self-care

Tooth extraction

  • If the tooth socket is bleeding:
    • Don't try to poke anything into the hole.
    • Fold a piece or clean,damp gauze or cotton cloth, like a handkerchief, into a pad and put it directly across the space where the toth was.
What to do if you start to feel worse
  1. call 111
  2. Tell them 111 online said you needed to get help now.
  3. Tell them your symptoms have got worse and you need to speak to a nurse.

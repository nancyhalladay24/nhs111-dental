Before you continue

The tooth needs to be reimplanted as soon as possible.

  • Don’t touch the root of the tooth. Only touch the part of the tooth that is usually visible.
  • If it's dirty, rinse gently in milk or under running water. Don’t scrub or clean with disinfectant.
  • Push the root of the tooth gently back in the socket as far as it will easily go.
  • Hold the tooth in place by gently biting on a piece of clean, folded cloth.

If you can't or don't want to re implant the tooth, carry the tooth in a container of milk. The tooth doesn't need to be cleaned before you put it in the milk.
